Win with Vivian

Vivian Jaber is a candidate for the 2025 city council election. She is running for the position of Ward 1 city council member. She is a strong believer in the power of the people. She is a strong advocate for social justice.

About Vivian

Leadership. Experiences. Values!

Vivian is a resilient community advocate dedicated to uplifting her community. She is a devoted mother of two and wife. Vivian balances her family responsibilities with community organizing, her nonprofit career and MBA studies at University of Oklahoma. As a Palestinian Muslim woman married to a Latino partner, she is deeply connected to the struggles of marginalized communities. With a BA in Political Science from California State University Northridge and expertise in Public Policy and Management, Vivian leverages her education and experiences to speak truth to power and advocate for those who are often unheard.

Housing is a fundamental human right essential to the dignity, safety and well being of our community. In OKC, our unhoused population is the most vulnerable members of our community; our vets, our youth, those experiencing mental illness, and our elderly population. Providing people with affordable housing provides a stable foundation for our community to thrive, supporting their access to education, employment and healthcare. Without secure housing, individuals face barriers that continue the cycle of poverty.

Obtaining real public safety in our community goes beyond traditional law enforcement. Instead, we must address the root causes of crime through comprehensive support and resources. Offering after school programs provides safe spaces for youth, keeping them engaged and out of trouble. Access to mental health resources helps individuals cope with trauma and stress, reducing the likelihood of crime.
